# Subscription Plan

## Subscription Process

> For more information about our membership plans, please refer to the [Membership Agreement](https://docs.chat2db.ai/en-US/docs/service/member)

First, you need to log in to your Chat2DB account.

If you have subscribed to a plan, you can log in to your Chat2DB account to use Chat2DB Pro during the subscription period.

You can check your subscription expiration date by **clicking on your avatar** or **viewing your personal information**. You can renew your plan again after your membership expires.

!["plan"](/image/start-guide/plan01.png)

Subscription plans are divided into the following two categories:

- **Personal Edition Membership**: Suitable for individual developers, data analysts.

- **Team Edition Membership**: Suitable for small project teams. 

!["plan"](/image/start-guide/plan02.png)

**If the system does not respond after the subscription is successful, just restart the app!**
