## Overview of MySQL Workbench

[MySQL Workbench](https://www.mysql.com/products/workbench/) is an integrated development environment (IDE) designed specifically for MySQL users. It offers functionality for database design, SQL development, administration, and data modeling. With its extensive features, MySQL Workbench helps streamline the process of managing and visualizing relational databases. However, while it caters to the requirements of many database professionals, it may not always be the most user-friendly or efficient solution for all users.

## Overview of Chat2DB
