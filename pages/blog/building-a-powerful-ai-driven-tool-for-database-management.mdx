One practical application of AI in database management is query optimization. The AI-driven tool can employ machine learning models to detect unusual patterns or outliers in data, flagging potential anomalies for further investigation. This proactive approach to anomaly detection enhances data security, mitigates risks, and safeguards the integrity of the database.
