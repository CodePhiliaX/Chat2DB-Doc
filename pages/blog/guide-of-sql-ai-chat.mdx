## Creating and Managing SQL AI Chat Queries

Creating effective queries using SQL AI Chat requires understanding how to structure natural language inquiries. Here are best practices for formulating queries that yield accurate results.

### Structuring Natural Language Queries

1. **Be Clear and Specific**: Use precise language to convey intent. Instead of asking, “What is the data?” specify, “What is the total revenue for Q1 2023?”
2. **Use Keywords**: Incorporate relevant keywords related to your data to help the AI understand your query better.
3. **Include Context**: Provide context if needed, such as, “List all customers who made purchases over $100 last month.”

### Example Queries and Optimization with Chat2DB

Chat2DB excels at optimizing these queries for efficiency. Here are a few examples demonstrating how to leverage its capabilities:

- **Query for Sales Data**:

```sql
SELECT customer_name, SUM(order_total) AS total_spent
FROM orders
WHERE order_date >= DATE_SUB(CURDATE(), INTERVAL 1 MONTH)
GROUP BY customer_name
ORDER BY total_spent DESC;
```

- **Query for Inventory Levels**:

```sql

FROM inventory
WHERE stock_level < 10;
```

Chat2DB can refine these queries, ensuring they execute efficiently and return results quickly. Users can avoid common pitfalls of poorly structured queries by utilizing Chat2DB’s suggestions and automations.
